Knitting... We've all seen it! Whether in real life or on film, the cliche of little old ladies knitting in rocking chairs or on the bus is not a rare sight. If you're like me, you sit and watch nonchalantly, but the gears in your head are turning - How does the yarn stay woven together? How does she alternate the colors so smoothly? How does she make the gaps and textures? - and I'm sure you've at least considered trying it for yourself.
Look no further, for I am here to save the day! This beginners' guide contains all you will need to learn how to knit. Trust me, it's simpler than you think... So come on in, and let's get knitting!
