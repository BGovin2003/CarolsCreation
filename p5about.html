About Us

Carol Jowers is a born-and-raised Tennesseean with a passion for crafting. She loves God, family, and making people smile--that's how Carol's Creations began. Over ten years ago, Carol realized that she could step beyond making DIY gifts for her friends and start her own little business selling these crafts she made in her spare time. Now she can make people all around smile, all while earning extra cash.
